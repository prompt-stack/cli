# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone.

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Focus on constructive feedback
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Prioritize what is best for the community and project

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
